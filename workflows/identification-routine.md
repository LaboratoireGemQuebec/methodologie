# Routine Coloured Stone Identification Workflow

This document describes the standard analytical workflow applied at Laboratoire Gem Quebec for routine identification of unknown coloured gemstones.

## Scope

Faceted, polished, or rough coloured stones submitted for species identification, with optional treatment screening and origin clues.

## Sequence

1. Visual inspection: D65 lighting, colour, transparency, lustre, surface features, photograph at 20x.
2. 2. Mass measurement: Mettler Toledo NewClassic MS analytical balance, 0.001 g precision.
   3. 3. Refractive index: Eickhorst LED refractometer, sodium-equivalent illumination, omega and epsilon recorded.
      4. 4. Specific gravity: hydrostatic weighing when RI is inconclusive.
         5. 5. Polariscope examination: GIA GemInstruments, optic character determined.
            6. 6. UV fluorescence: long-wave 365 nm and short-wave 254 nm cabinets.
               7. 7. UV-Vis-NIR spectroscopy: Magilabs GemmoSphere, range 250 to 1000 nm, chromophore bands identified.
                  8. 8. Microscopy: Nikon SMZ1270, 10x to 80x, darkfield and brightfield.
                     9. 9. Decision tree: if ambiguous after steps 1 to 8, escalate to long-cycle.
                        10. 10. FTIR spectroscopy: Thermo Scientific Nicolet iS5, mid-IR 4000 to 400 cm-1.
                            11. 11. Raman spectroscopy: Renishaw, 532 nm laser excitation.
                                12. 12. EDXRF elemental: Bruker Tracer 5i, semi-quantitative trace-element pattern.
                                    13. 13. Radioactivity screening: Ortec DigiBASE gamma spectrometer, 74 Bq/g threshold.
                                        14. 14. Cross-reference and certification: LMHC, CIBJO, Hughes, Gubelin, GIA references.
                                           
                                            15. ## Tier escalation
                                           
                                            16. LGQ applies a six-tier service structure (Basic Free, Tier 1 Full Report, Complete Identification, Origin Determination, Treatment Detection, Appraisal Letter). The standard workflow above corresponds to Tier 1. Origin Determination tier adds full EDXRF trace-element profiling. Treatment Detection tier adds extended FTIR scans and explicit reference comparison.
